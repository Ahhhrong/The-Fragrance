Who We Are

fragrance Pte Ltd was born in 2002 with the simple concept of retailing everyday products. We worked hard at understanding our customers, sourcing for products that fit different needs and budgets.

Since then, our company has grown to have 20 stores in operation, each holding over 5,000 products. Included in our offerings are cosmetics, skin care, hair care, body care, and fragrances. We also carry household cleaning products, some confectionaries, as well as specialty seasonal items.

fragrance is the local distributor of several imported brands, some of which are exclusive to our stores. We have also come to develop and feature a number of in-house brands.



Why Choose Us

INDUSTRY EXPERTISE

We have a team of experienced sales people, all of whom are knowledgeable and skilled in delivering outstanding customer service. Our retail outlets hold a variety of brands, so we do not have allegiance to any which one in particular. UPGRADED STORAGE

Our storage facilities are equipped with proper ventilation systems.
